SkankyApp

Audio loops for rehearsals — install it once, use it anywhere

Open this address in your browser:

https://skanky-web.vercel.app/

📱 iPhone — Install

  1. 1
    Open Safari Must be Safari — Chrome won't let you install it on iPhone
  2. 2
    Go to the address above Type or paste it into the Safari address bar
  3. 3
    Tap the Share button The box with an arrow pointing up — at the bottom of the screen
  4. 4
    Tap "Add to Home Screen" Scroll down the share menu if you can't see it, then tap Add
  5. 5
    Open the app from your home screen Always open it from the icon — not from Safari

🤖 Android — Install

  1. 1
    Open Chrome Works best in Chrome on Android
  2. 2
    Go to the address above
  3. 3
    Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) Top right corner of Chrome
  4. 4
    Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" Then tap Add or Install when prompted
  5. 5
    Open the app from your home screen

🎵 Load the Loops

Before you start: make sure the audio files and the three CSV files (loops.csv, bands.csv, setlists.csv) are somewhere you can find on your phone — iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or the Files app all work fine.
  1. 1
    Open the app, tap Settings (bottom right)
  2. 2
    Tap "Add Audio Files" Select all the loop audio files at once — you can select multiple files
  3. 3
    Tap "Import CSV" Select all three files at once: loops.csv, bands.csv, and setlists.csv
  4. 4
    Tap Loops — your setlists and loops should all be there Use the dropdown at the top to switch between setlists
Tip: Tap a loop's title to expand it and see chords and notes. Tap the speaker icon on the right to play or stop it. The ↺ badge next to a band name means that loop will repeat until you stop it.
